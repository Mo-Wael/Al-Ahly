These were some of the most famous phrases uttered by Al-Ahly Sports Club fans. Al-Ahly Club is an Egyptian sports club that was established by a number of senior Egyptian figures such as Talaat Harb Pasha, Mustafa Kamel, Ali Lotfi Bey and Amin Sami Pasha during the English colonization of Egypt.

The club was founded in 1907, and the first board of directors for the club was established, headed by Englishman Michael Innes, to be the first president of the club.

The football club was established in Al-Ahly Club in 1909, which at that time consisted of primary and middle school students who were playing the game at the time on the floor of Al-Hawsh Stadium (currently Al-Touch), which was established in 1911.

The first team consisted of:

  • Hussein Fawzy
  • Ibrahim Othman
  • Muhammad Bakri
  • Suleiman Fayek
  • Hassan Muhammad
  • Ahmed Fouad Anwar(the team's first captain)
  • Hussein Hegazy(the team's striker)
  • Abdel Fattah Taher
  • Fouad Darwish
  • Hussein Mansour
  • Ibrahim Fahmy

The first championship that the club achieved was the Sultan Hussein Cup in 1923, and then the Egypt Cup title in 1924, and it was the first participation in the Egyptian League championship in 1948, which Al-Ahly club won, to be the beginning of the long journey of achieving all local and African championships.

And the beginning of Al-Ahly's march in the African Championships was in 1976, but they did not have success in that version, as the team exited from the first round in addition to the following version, from which the team exited from the quarter-finals, and in 1982 the club achieved the first title of the African Champions League and be This is the beginning of a record number of 10 titles (so far)

Here are some of the club's most famous titles:

  • 42 titles of Egyptian League
  • 37 titles of Egyptian Cup
  • 10 titles of African champions league
  • 5 participations in the Club World Cup (3 bronze medals)
  • The second club with the most international trophies (behind Real Madrid)
